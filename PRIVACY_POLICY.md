# Privacy Policy for Emplates

**Last Updated:** December 24, 2025

## Overview

Emplates ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data. 

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers.**

### What Data is Stored Locally

- **Email Templates**: The templates you create (template names and body text) are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's sync storage. 
- **Extension Settings**: Your preference for enabling/disabling the extension is stored locally. 

### Chrome Sync Storage

If you have Chrome Sync enabled in your browser, your templates will sync across your devices signed into the same Google account.  This syncing is handled entirely by Google Chrome, not by us.  We do not have access to this data.

## Permissions

The Extension requests the following permission: 

- **Storage**: Used to save your email templates locally in your browser. 

### Content Scripts

The Extension runs on the following websites to provide its functionality: 
- mail.google.com (Gmail)
- outlook.live.com (Outlook)
- outlook.office.com (Outlook)
- outlook.office365.com (Outlook)

**We do NOT:**
- Read your emails
- Collect email addresses or contact information
- Track your browsing activity
- Send any data to external servers
- Use analytics or tracking tools

## Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools. 

## Data Security

All your data remains on your device and within your Chrome browser's storage. We do not have access to your data. 

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device: 
- You can delete all your templates at any time through the extension's options page
- You can uninstall the extension at any time, which will remove all associated data
- You have complete control over your data
